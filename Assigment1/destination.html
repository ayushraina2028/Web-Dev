Savandurga Trek


About:

One of the best treks near Banglore. It is a great place for trekking and rock climbing. It is a great place for one day trip. It also has a temple at the top of the hill.

Location:

It is located near Magadi, Ramanagara district, Karnataka, India. It is 60 km from Banglore.

Climate:

The climate is very pleasant. It is a great place to visit in winter season. It is not recommended to visit in rainy season to avoid any accidents.

Attractions:

There are a lot of things to do in Savandurga. There are some of best things to see like:

A Cool View:

Before starting the trek, we can see the beauty of the mountain range from ground itself. We can see how beautiful it is. Also this is Karnataka's 15th highest peak.

Fort Wall:

One of the steepest part is this fortwall. You may feel your trekking very easy till this point but this part is very difficult. This needs to be climbed up and down properly and slowly to avoid accidents. There is availability of slight footholds also.

Pond:

Another beautiful attraction is this little pond which you will find after completing around 80-85% of your trek. This is a great place to take rest and enjoy the beauty of nature. Best thing is to click pictures here with your team.

Nandi Temple at Top:

After finding the above pond, you will be able to see the Nandi Temple from there. Now is the final portion of the Trek, that you will complete to reach at top.

Here is close picture of the Nandi Statue

Accomodations:

Currently there are no Accomodations available there, because the trek is located in a forest area. There are some Accomodations available in Magadi district which is 10 km away from Savandurga. Usually this is a one day trip for Banglore people so they do not book Accomodations.

Travel Tips:

  1. Visit During Week Days to avoid more crowd
  2. Bring energy bars and drinks along with you
  3. Wear comfortable clothes and shoes
  4. Do not litter

Safety Precautions:

  1. Shoes are must
  2. Do not go in rainy season
  3. Do not go in night
  4. Visit in small Groups
